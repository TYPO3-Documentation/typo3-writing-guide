Contributing Content to TYPO3
-----------------------------

As an open source project, TYPO3 accepts content contributions of all
kinds, including, but not limited to the following areas:

-  **Articles and blog posts for the websites .org and .com**

   -  To contribute to typo3.org, read `where to submit the content
      requests <https://docs.google.com/document/d/1kaWoSm05SX-ZWGDkY9sEWb-1GWvDmfrNd_5pzPvUGaE/edit\#heading=h.e271qw6rcm4s>`__ section
      of the TYPO3 Community Content Workflow.
   -  To submit to typo3.com, get in touch via the `typo3.com contact
      form <https://typo3.com/contact>`__.

-  **Official documentation:** We can always use help fixing issues,
   reviewing pull requests, writing new content, adding diagrams,
   creating cheat sheets, replacing outdated images, adding YouTube
   videos, reviewing manuals, and more.
   For more information and instructions, visit the `How You Can
   Help <https://docs.typo3.org/m/typo3/docs-how-to-document/master/en-us/WritingDocsOfficial/HowYouCanHelp.html\#docs-official-how-you-can-help>`__ page
   in our documentation.
-  **Graphics and Design:** Join our `Design
   Team <https://typo3.org/community/teams/design>`__’s hangouts or
   sprints to help improve the TYPO3 brand design.
-  **Marketing Team:** Join the `Marketing
   Team <https://typo3.org/community/teams/marketing>`__ and begin
   contributing to our sales and marketing resources.
-  **CoCoMOn:** The Collaborative Communication, Marketing, and
   Onboarding `project <https://typo3.org/article/come-on-join-cocomon>`__ produces
   an open source marketing and educational toolkit for agencies around
   the world.
-  **Podcast:** Share your knowledge, suggest topics or guests `via
   email <podcasts@openstrategypartners.com>`_ for Application,
   the TYPO3 Community Podcast, or host TYPO3 community members on your
   own.
-  **TYPO3 Core:** Join the `Core
   Team <https://typo3.org/community/teams/typo3-development>`__ to
   help shape the product’s UI and write microcopy.

.. _2-community-contributing-to-this-guide:

Contributing to this Guide
--------------------------

Send your suggestions, feedback, examples, or other ideas to us. The
Editorial Team of this guide will review submissions once per quarter
and get in touch with you or take other appropriate actions.
